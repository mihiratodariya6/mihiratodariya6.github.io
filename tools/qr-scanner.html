Most smartphone cameras have built-in scanners, but what if you receive a QR code in an email, a PDF, or while browsing on your laptop? ToolMitra's QR Code Scanner bridges that gap. Built with robust HTML5 WebRTC technology, it turns any browser into a high-speed barcode and QR reader.
