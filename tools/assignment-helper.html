How to Use AI for Assignments Ethically

Generative AI is transforming global education. However, academic integrity is paramount. ToolMitra's AI Assignment Helper is designed as an ideation and structuring framework, not an essay-writing bypass. By generating a robust skeleton, students can overcome writer's block, structure arguments logically, and focus their time on deep research and original writing.

Logical Structuring

Our engine breaks down complex prompts into digestible sections: Introduction, Body Paragraph arguments (with counter-points), and definitive Conclusions.

Thesis Generation

Struggling with the main argument? The tool formulates strong, debatable thesis statement ideas based on your academic level and format.

Research Targeting

The AI provides targeted Google Scholar and JSTOR search queries to help you find credible, peer-reviewed sources faster.
