Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

At ToolMitra, accessible from our website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by ToolMitra and how we use it.

1. Local Processing & Data Security

We pride ourselves on providing a secure digital environment. The vast majority of our utilities—including PDF mergers, image compressors, and AI text tools—utilize Client-Side Processing (HTML5 Canvas & WebAssembly). This means your files, documents, and images are processed directly within your browser. We do not upload, store, or view your sensitive files on our servers.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account to use our basic free tools. However, to improve user experience, we collect standard, non-personally identifiable information automatically via log files. This includes:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) addresses
  • Browser type and version
  • Internet Service Provider (ISP)
  • Date and time stamps
  • Referring/exit pages

3. Google AdSense & Cookies

ToolMitra uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. LocalStorage Usage

We use your browser's localStorage to save your UI preferences, such as Dark Mode settings and your "Recently Used Tools" history. This data remains strictly on your personal device and is never transmitted to ToolMitra.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to Contact Us.
